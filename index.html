01 · The map

Who's home on a given day

Each pin is a school, colored to match it, with everyone you've got there grouped underneath. Click one pin, then another within 10 seconds, to measure the distance and driving time between them. Scrub the date down in "The year" below to see who's home.

02 · On the ground

Every campus, on a real map

Actual coordinates, no decluttering. Click a pin for who's there and where, then click another within 10 seconds to measure the distance and driving time between them.

03 · The year

Every break, side by side

Break windows are filled in automatically from real 2026–27 academic calendars for the schools we've verified, and a reasonable estimate everywhere else — matched by school name, not guaranteed exact. Drag the slider to scrub through the year; the line moves across every strip and the map above updates too.
